About me

Hello World! I'm Laura, a 29 year old mixed Mediterrean German human being based in Berlin. After having done a Bachelor of Arts in Cultural Science and Philosophy during which I have gained work experience as a Receptionist in one of Berlin's best rated Hostels, I have continued a Career in Tourism working as an Office Manager for a Bike Rental & Bike Tour Operator.

Since always however I've had a thing for logical thinking and Technology which led me to start volunteering in autumn 2020 as a "technical coach" for the Elderly. As I seem to have an enjoyable personality and have been doing good work, I have been proposed to lead a Windows Beginner Course for the Elderly which I have gladly accepted and doing at the moment.

So.. when and how did Programming come into my Life? I have had the dormant plan for years which I started putting into practice after breaking my fibula and being constricted to not move for 6 weeks. With this perspective I instantly started an online Career Path to become a Full-Stack Developer by the wonderful e-Learning platform CodeCademy and have been loving the challenge!
